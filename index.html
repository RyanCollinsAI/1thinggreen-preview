Questions, answered.

Will it fit my trash can?
It fits cans 8 to 13 inches across the top, which covers most standard kitchen and drawer cans. Measure straight across the opening to check.
Is it sturdy enough to hold the bags?
Yes. The can band locks the bag and the patented top brace keeps both compartments rigid. As one reviewer put it, "the top brace is effective at keeping the compartments stay in place."
Compost or recycling — which bundle do I want?
Both bundles are the same patented divider. The Recycling Bundle comes with reusable can bands and recycling labels. The Compost Bundle comes with compostable bags and compost labels.
What's it made of?
Recycled plastic, made in the USA. The included compost bags are compostable.
How fast does it ship?
Jill packs and ships orders in 1-2 business days. Free shipping over $30.
Do I line both sides, or just one?
Each side gets its own bag, held by a can band. Most people put compost or recycling on one side and regular trash on the other. If you only need one extra stream, line a single side and leave the other open.
What if a bag tears or slips down?
The can band holds each bag over the rim so it doesn't slide as it fills. If a bag tears, swap it the way you would in any can. The divider and top brace stay put.
Does it smell? How do I keep odor down?
It's the same as any kitchen bin, just split in two. For the compost side, pull the bag every couple of days and keep the lid closed. The recycled plastic wipes clean and doesn't hold odor the way a fabric caddy does.
Can I switch from recycling to compost later?
Yes. It's the same divider either way. Swap the labels and use the compostable bags for food scraps instead of recyclables. Plenty of people run compost on one side and recycling on the other.
What's your return policy?
Every order is covered by a 30-day money-back guarantee. If it doesn't fix your two-bin problem, reach out within 30 days and we'll refund you. Jill handles returns herself.
